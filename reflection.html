Progress Assessment

Right now, my portfolio is coming along very well. It has all the main sections I need, like my education, work experience, and skills. I still need to add the three major projects for this class, but I am really happy with how the site looks so far. The color scheme feels right, it is easy to navigate, and the fonts are very clear to read. Building this site from scratch with HTML taught me a lot. I used to think a portfolio was this super complicated thing with tons of charts and advanced projects that only seniors had. Now I realize it is basically just a better way to show off your resume where you actually have the space to explain your work, no matter where you are in college.

My biggest goal now, and what I am finding most difficult is figuring out how to make the site more unique. I want it to look professional for recruiters, but I also want it to show my actual personality as an engineer and a musician. I am still figuring out exactly how to do that, so I plan to look at other people's portfolios to see what makes them stand out. By seeing what works and what doesn't, I can edit my site to make sure it feels like me while still looking like a serious professional.
